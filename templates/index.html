Welcome to our Deepfake Image Detector! We aim to provide a tool that helps users identify whether an image is real or fake using advanced AI techniques. Our commitment is to contribute to the fight against misinformation and fake content.

Provide the Input Image

Our project addresses the increasing concern of image security in today's digital environment, particularly in industries like healthcare and surveillance. Unauthorized access and manipulation of images pose serious risks, leading to potential errors in medical diagnoses and undermining public safety. Conventional protection techniques often fall short, necessitating cutting-edge tactics to close gaps and prevent misinformation. Our focus is on developing robust image security measures using innovative technologies such as blockchain integration and artificial intelligence algorithms. By ensuring the authenticity of visual information, we aim to foster a digital environment where trust, reliability, and societal stability are paramount, contributing to a global ecosystem of transparency and innovation.

Objectives

The project objectives are to implement deep learning models like CNN and transfer learning for distinguishing real from fake images, analyze their performance using metrics such as accuracy, precision, recall, and F1 score, and conduct a comparative analysis of these models.
